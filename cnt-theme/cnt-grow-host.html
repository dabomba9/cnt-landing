What's a typical transaction?

For Hosts (Property Owners)

A typical transaction with CurbNTurf, a platform for RVers to find and book properties to park their RVs, usually involves the following steps:

1. listing Your property
  • Sign Up: Register on the CurbNTurf platform.
  • Create a Listing: Provide details about your property, including location, amenities, photos, and pricing.
  • Set Availability: Specify the dates and times when your property is available for RVers.
2. Receiving Booking Requests:
  • Guests browse listings and send booking requests for dates they wish to stay.
  • Hosts receive notifications of these requests and can review the details.
3. Approving or Declining Requests:
  • Hosts can approve or decline booking requests based on their availability or preference.
  • Upon approval, the reservation is confirmed.
4. Guest Arrival and Stay:
  • Hosts prepare the property for the guest's arrival.
  • They may greet the guests, offer a brief tour, and provide any necessary information about the property.
5. Completion of Stay:
  • After the guest’s stay, the host ensures the property is left in good condition.
6. Receiving Payment:
  • Payment for the stay is processed through CurbNTurf, typically after the guest’s arrival or at the end of the stay.
  • Hosts receive their earnings, minus any fees charged by CurbNTurf.

Is my property a good fit?

Discover the Potential of Your Space.

All that's required to host is access and a somewhat level parking spot. At CurbNTurf, we believe every property has something unique to offer. Whether you have a sprawling rural landscape, a cozy backyard in the city, or a scenic spot near a popular destination, your space could be the next go-to stop for the vibrant RVing community. Wondering if your property is suitable for hosting RVers? Let's explore together!

Evaluate Your Space
1. location, location, Location
  • Urban Areas: Even in the city, a small driveway or backyard can be a perfect overnight haven for travelers.
  • Countryside Charm: Rural lands offer the serenity and space that RVers often seek.
  • Nearby Attractions: Proximity to tourist spots, national parks, state parks, or unique local attractions adds value.
2. Amenities:
  • Basic Offering: Do you have a flat space where an RV can park? That's a great start!
  • Hookups: While water, electric, and sewer hookups are not required, if you have them, fantastic.
  • Nearby Attractions: Proximity to tourist spots, natural parks, or unique local attractions adds value.
3. Your Desire to connect:
  • Are you comfortable with guests staying on your property?
  • Today's RV community is looking for connection to unique people and places.  You may even have a hobby or specialty to share.  Let's connect.  Join the CurbNTurf family today!
