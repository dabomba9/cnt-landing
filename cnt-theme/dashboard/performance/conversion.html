Booking Conversion

Overall conversion rate is the average daily number of distinct visitors who viewed your listing in search and then booked.

Conversion has 3 stages:
1. A guest views your listing in search. Some guests will view it on the first page of search results. (First-page search impressions)
2. The guest then clicks through to your listing page after they see it in search results. (Search-to-listing conversion)
3. The guest books your listing after viewing it. (Listing-to-booking conversion)
Why it's important
Conversion may indicate how appealing and relevant your listings may be to travelers hoping to book. Successful conversions depend on many factors, including pricing, policies, ratings, booking settings, and listing content

returning guests

Returning guests is the percent of your guests who have previously stayed at any of your listings.

We divide the total number of returning guests by the total number of all guests for each listing and average that number based on how many listings you have.

Why it's important
When a guest returns, it may mean their previous stay with you was great.

VIEWS

The number of distinct visitors who saw the listing page when searching for trip dates within your selected dates, during the 90 days leading up to those days.

Why it's important
When page views are low, some hosts may choose to take action, like lowering prices for a set of dates, to increase the chances of guests viewing a listing during that time.

Favorites Additions

Wishlist additions is the average number of times your listings are added to guest wishlists.

For individual listings, we show you the total number of times the listing was added to a wishlist.

Why it's important
When a guest wishlists a listing, it may mean that it was appealing to them and suitable for their stay.
