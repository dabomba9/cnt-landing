Site Description

This site is located in Idaho high desert. But in a valley with a view of irrigated farmland and natural high desert hills where cattle graze. The area is gets greener in April with the end of winter and rain and then turns golden as the rains end. Sagebrush are more common than trees. The site is home to many pheasants, quails, whistle pigs, badgers, hawks, owls, and coyotes. As a bonus, you will be 14 miles to Paddock Valley Reservoir for those who fish or boat. In addition, We are 4 miles away from BLM land used for recreational UTV riding, 8 miles for BLM land for horseback riding, trail based UTV, and shooting. Or come to just relax. During the day, the shop has employees working inside.
