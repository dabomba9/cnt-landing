CurbNTurf charges $5 minimum fee to the guest to cover the overhead and other cost associated to providing the best service and platform possible for our customers.

What is the guest feedback deposit? As a host, you want feedback to encourage new guest to your site. The deposit from the guest(s) encourages them to give feedback from their stay. At the end of the guest's stay, they'll receive a follow up message asking them to rate and provide feedback about their stay and the site. Once the guest has filled out this feedback they'll receive all the deposit back in the form of CurbNTurf Cash.

What is CurbNTurf Cash? This is the transactional debits and credits internally on CurbNTurf. CurbNTurf Cash is designed to bring guest(s) back. Guest(s) will use their CurbNTurf Cash with future reservations helping bring more guests to you.
