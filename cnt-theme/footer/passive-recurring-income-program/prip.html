Helping Entrepreneurial Property Owners

At CurbNTurf we help entrepreneurial property owners make more income by leveraging a platform that connects RVers to landowners. We are passionate about helping landowners monetize their property while providing unique and memorable experiences for their guests.

As a host, simply post your listing on the CurbNTurf site. CurbNTurf lists and promotes your property as an opportunity for guests —RV travelers— to reserve your property. Guests can search CurbNTurf using desired search features to plan their trip and pay for their stay.

How you can make recurring income with referrals?

Through our Passive Recurring Income Program (PRIP), CurbNTurf wants to reward you for helping us grow our community. Simply refer a friend or family member to our service to start earning. 

Here's how it works:

Invite a friend to become a host on CurbNTurf

Verify - We’llyour referral becomes a host and lists their property

Book - Once your referred host books and completes any guest’s stay, you start making money

Earn - Earn 3%* commission on every total reservation cost, for the lifetime of the published listing (20% of CurbNTurf’s fee).

We have the right to cancel transactions if there is any evidence of fraud.
