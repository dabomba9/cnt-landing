CurbNTurf Cash & Account Balance

CurbNTurf Cash: CurbNTurf Cash” is the debit and credit transactional history for Guest’s related user account who earn CurbNTurf Cash from reviews or any issued refunds. Any CurbNTurf Cash balance is automatically used on CurbNTurf as the first payment method toward reservations.

A user may, at any time, withdraw CurbNTurf Cash in US Dollars (One CurbNTurf Cash Equals One US Dollar). Any withdrawal is subject to a five-dollar processing fee.

Account Balance: “Account Balance” is the debit and credit transactional history for users who generate income from reservation transactions. Upon a successfully completed reservation transaction, the user’s portion of the income will be deposited into this user-related account. A user may withdraw from this account at any time. This account is recorded in US Dollars. Withdraws made are calculated toward the 1099 MISC tax form. Any combined withdrawals per calendar year reaching the minimum 1099 MISC reporting limit will trigger a 1099 MISC form. Upon reaching this 1099 trigger, a user will be asked for their social security number so that payments equal to or over the minimum limit can be sent to the user.

Pricing, Payouts, & Fees

Host’s Private Listing: CurbNTurf’s calculator adds the following surcharges to the Host defined take home price per site per night: 1.) The greater of $5 fee or 15% commission fee; and, 2.) $5 CurbNTurf Cash Guest Incentive.

Upon the completion of a reservation and stay CurbNTurf receives a portion of the financial transaction as commission for its services as the commission fee outlined above.

Upon the completion of a reservation, stay, and leaving an honest experience review the Guest receives the CurbNTurf Cash Guest Incentive portion of the transaction outlined above. This is received by the Guest as CurbNTurf Cash and is designed to be an incentive used toward future reservations.

Boondocking: To support the going concern of CurbNTurf, donations to virtual bookings at Boondocking sites are split between the listing user and CurbNTurf equally 50/50. The user will receive their donated portion as CurbNTurf cash.

Cancellation & Refund policy

Refunds - All refunds are applied to your CurbNTurf Cash account. Cash withdrawals can be made from this account and incur a $5 processing fee for each request. Otherwise, the CurbNTurf Cash is automatically applied to your next reservation.

Easy Goin' - Free Cancellation - Cancel up to one day prior to check-in* to get a full refund. Any early check-outs will receive a full refund for all nights falling 24 hours after cancellation.

Moderate - Free Cancellation -  Cancel up to three days prior to check-in* to get a full refund. Any early check-outs will receive a half refund for all nights falling 24 hours after cancellation.

Strict - Partial Refund -  Cancel up to seven days prior to check-in* to get a half refund. There are no refunds for early check-outs.

Exclusive - Non-Refundable -  Any cancellation is non-refundable. There are no refunds for early check-outs.

*Check-in refers to your listing's specified check-in time (or 2 pm if not specified) on day of check-in.

A “Completed” Reservation is a booking accepted by the Referred Host and paid for by the Guest and is not canceled by either the Guest or the Referred Host at any time before or after the scheduled check-in for such booking.

Through the PRIP, after the affiliate’s referral has listed their host site and a guest has completed their stay, the affiliate will receive three percent (3%) of the Reservation for the lifetime of the published listing.
