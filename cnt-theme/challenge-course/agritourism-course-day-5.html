Day 5: Hosting Guests on Your Property.

By offering your home or other places on your property, you can host guests to encourage longer stay periods and create more value. However, if you are not comfortable with Airbnb hosting or don't want the maintenance or risk, there is an additional way to generate income from your property through CurbNTurf.

CurbNTurf is a listing place for RVers who are looking for a place to park. Property owners and agritourism businesses can list their property on CurbNTurf and rent it out to RVers for a fee.

In today's video, we will discuss how you can capitalize on this opportunity and use your property to generate even more income. We’ll also look into the process of setting up the listing on CurbNTurf and what you need to know before hosting RVer guests on your property.
