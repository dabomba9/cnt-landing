Scotch Pines Golf Course

Award winning 18 Hole public golf course, built in 1963, with full bar/restaurant, driving range, practice green and instructional programs. Course is very walkable, with beautiful surroundings, no home sites, 4 sets of tees for all level of players and priced very affordable. Open all year round (weather permitting), normally Feb through Nov.
